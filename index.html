PastÃ©is de Nata

Custard tarts with flaky pastry and caramelized tops.

Ingredients

  • Puff pastry
  • 500 ml milk
  • 6 egg yolks
  • 150 g sugar
  • 40 g flour
  • Lemon peel, cinnamon stick

Method

  1. Infuse milk with lemon & cinnamon; remove.
  2. Thicken milk with sugar & flour; off heat, add yolks.
  3. Fill pastry-lined tins and bake very hot until blistered.
  4. Dust with cinnamon and serve.
